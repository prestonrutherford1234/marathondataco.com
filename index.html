The Problems

Your tools weren't built for brands at your stage

Most analytics platforms optimize for volume—smaller brands, simpler questions. Scaling brands need sophistication, granularity, and integration that doesn't exist elsewhere.

01

"Is my brand spending actually doing anything?"

You have MTA tools. MMM tools. In-platform metrics. Incrementality tests. But they all disagree—and none of them measure brand. So top-of-funnel remains a black box. You're spending millions on faith.

Marathon Solution: Triangulated Brand Measurement—unified attribution across MTA, MMM, and incrementality with codified weights you can audit. Run tests. Verify predictions. Finally, measurement you can trust because you can test it.
02

"Why can't I connect my website to actual profit?"

GA shows pageviews. Shopify shows orders. But which products drive the best contribution margin? Which lead to high-LTV customers? Where should you send traffic? What inventory should you buy? The data lives in silos that never answer the real questions.

Marathon Solution: Web Analytics → Financial Metrics—the first system connecting website behavior to product-level contribution margin, customer LTV, and new vs. repeat. Know exactly where to send traffic, what products to prioritize, and what inventory to buy.
03

"Scaling creative requires scaling headcount"

You know which products perform. You know which pages convert. But turning those insights into campaigns still requires an army of people. AI tools promise scale, but without real data, they just produce slop faster.

Marathon Solution: Intelligence-Driven Auto-Generation—auto-generate ads and campaigns triggered by your actual product economics. High-LTV products. Winning pages. Contribution margin signals. Not random AI—creative that's informed by what actually drives profit.
04

"Why is annual planning such a nightmare?"

Planning takes months. Forecasts are guesswork. You can't model "what if we cut brand spend" or "what if we launch this product" because the underlying data isn't connected. So you build spreadsheets on top of spreadsheets and pray.

Marathon Solution: Connected Growth Modeling—because all your data flows through one system, model any scenario and get outputs in incremental contribution margin and LTV. Not vanity metrics. The actual numbers your CFO cares about.
